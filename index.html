Botanical Search

How to do the quiz:

  1. The questions are all based around pictures taken in botanical gardens all around the world!
  2. You will be asked to find something in each picture.
  3. Examine the picture and count how many of them you can find.
  4. Select the answer that you think is correct.
  5. Click the next button to load the next question.
  6. Try and be as fast as you can, here is a timer that will track how long you take.
  7. After attempting all of the questions, you will be shown your score and how long it took.
  8. You can retry the game to beat your time or get all answers correct.
  9. Have fun and enjoy the botanical search!
